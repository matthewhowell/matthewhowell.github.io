Cameras

Most of these photos (nearly all of the recent ones) were taken with my Fuji X100F ↗ or my previous Fuji X100S ↗. Alternatively, for something a bit different, my Positives project used a Polaroid SX-70 instant camera.

Alt Text

A few of these photos were included in my 2021 project Alt Text, which was an effort to write longer photograph descriptions in the form of short essays about their larger context. So, in that spirit, each new photo here will (eventually) also include a similar short essay in addition to its standard, descriptive alt text.
