Read more cameras

Most of these photos (nearly all of the recent ones) were taken with my Fuji X100F or my previous Fuji X100S. Alternatively, something a bit different, my [Positives](/work/positives) project used a Polaroid SX-70 instant camera.

A few of these photos were included in my 2021 project Alt Text, which was an effort to write longer photograph descriptions in the form of short essays about their context. So, in that spirit, each new photo includes a similar short essay in addition to its standard, descriptive alt text.
