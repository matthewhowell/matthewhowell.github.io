Read more about the star ratings

Each book listed below includes a very subjective star rating. 0 stars for books that I did not enjoy. 1 star for books that I enjoyed, 2 stars for books that I very much enjoyed, and 3 stars for books that I loved.

Read more about yearly statistics

Since 2015, I've logged 6 books.

3 (about 50%) of those books were written by women and 0 (about 0%) were written by people of color. Simply keeping those statistics has helped me improve those percentages quite a bit. This reading log is a tool to help me read more and also to read more diverse authors.
