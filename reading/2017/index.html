Reading/2017

Ratings

Each book listed below includes a very subjective star rating. The scale here is small and defined to make it easier to rate each book. I also reference these ratings whenever it's time to clear some bookshelf space and donate books.

  •  
    0 stars for books that I did not enjoy
  • ★
    1 star for books that I enjoyed
  • ★★
    2 stars for books that I very much enjoyed
  • ★★★
    3 stars for books that I loved

Statistics

Since 2015, I've logged 9 books.

2 (about 22%) of those books were written by women and 4 (about 44%) were written by people of color. Simply keeping those statistics has helped me improve those percentages quite a bit. This reading log is a tool to help me read more and also to read more diverse authors.
