Reading

/Reading is an ongoing list of books that I've read, organized by year, in approximate chronological order.

The list is mostly fiction, because I read mostly fiction. It intentionally omits technical/programming books because those don't seem interesting to track. And I only log books that I finish, since I can't very well rate a book that I didn't complete.

This isn't really a set of recommendations, it's primarily a tool to encourage myself to read more. I love to read and books are probably my favorite objects in the world. Fiction can be nourishing, transportive, and something resembling magic.

Read more about the star ratings

Each book listed below includes a very subjective star rating. 0 stars for books that I did not enjoy. 1 star for books that I enjoyed, 2 stars for books that I very much enjoyed, and 3 stars for books that I loved.

Read more about yearly statistics

Since 2015, I've logged 90 books.

57 (about 63%) of those books were written by women and 37 (about 41%) were written by people of color. Simply keeping those statistics has helped me improve those percentages quite a bit. This reading log is a tool to help me read more and also to read more diverse authors.
