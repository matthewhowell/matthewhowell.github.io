Reading

Here, find a list of (mostly) fiction that I’ve read (or reread) recently; approximately last in, first out. Everything listed is something that I’ve finished, and so have probably enjoyed. Highlights indicate titles that I particularly loved, or recommend, or ones that I recall most vividly and positively from that year.

In addition to the yearly lists below, you’ll find a smaller list of capital F Favorite books. This group doesn’t change often, but it will get an occasional addition. It’s less a set of recommendations and more just books that have brought me some kind of joy. Fiction can be nourishing, transportive, and just plain magical. Magical!

Statistics

Since 2015, the math says I am reading an average of 10.00 books each year. The purpose of this log is, primarily, to help me read more. So, the goal for this year is to read more than 10 books.

The second purpose of this log is to help me read more books written by women and authors of color. Currently, the list below is comprised of 62% ladies and 40% authors of color. The goal is to be above 50% for each going forward.
