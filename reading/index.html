Reading

/Reading is an ongoing list of books that I've read, organized by year, in approximate chronological order.

The list is mostly fiction, because I read mostly fiction. It intentionally omits technical/programming books because those don't seem interesting to track. And I only log books that I finish, since I can't very well rate a book that I didn't complete.

This isn't really a set of recommendations, it's primarily a tool to encourage myself to read more. I love to read and books are probably my favorite objects in the world. Fiction can be nourishing, transportive, and something resembling magic.

Ratings

Each book listed below includes a very subjective star rating. The scale here is small and defined to make it easier to rate each book. I also reference these ratings whenever it's time to clear some bookshelf space and donate books.

  •  
    0 stars for books that I did not enjoy
  • ★
    1 star for books that I enjoyed
  • ★★
    2 stars for books that I very much enjoyed
  • ★★★
    3 stars for books that I loved

Statistics

Since 2015, I've logged 124 books.

80 (about 64%) of those books were written by women and 49 (about 39%) were written by people of color. Simply keeping those statistics has helped me improve those percentages quite a bit. This reading log is a tool to help me read more and also to read more diverse authors.
