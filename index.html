Matthew Howell is a person trying to make nice websites and–sometimes–other things.

Hello, friend! My name is Matthew, this is my personal website, and I believe websites are amazing. This one is an attempt at some kind of public memory. There is a bit of writing collected in /notes, some /photos, and a catch-all accumulation of my other /work. There is also a /reading log, and a very, very (really very) occasional email newsletter called ▲ Pizza.

For professional inquiries, find me over at Reasonable Company, my values-led design and creative practice, where I attempt to do good work for humans and screens.

Find more about me and this website by clicking this cool /about hyperlink or say hello over on Mastodon.
