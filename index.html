Hello, gentle Internet person. Welcome to matthewhowell.net, a small website that, I can assure you, exists for several reasons.

And what does one do here?

First, perhaps, one could subscribe to my email newsletter, Pizza, which has been very occasionally delivering emails about technology and other things since 2015.

Next, there’s a bit of writing, collected in Notes. It’s not so much, just a handful of small, personal essays that tend to focus on technology and other things.

For the very, very bored, there are smaller and more timely updates available in Updates. The most recent entry is always available at /updates/latest. A public list of fiction books that I’ve managed to read recently can be found in Reading.

And finally, for those readers who have exhausted all of the text on this page, About contains paragraphs upon paragraphs of more words.
