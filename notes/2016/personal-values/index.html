Personal Values

February 23, 2016

A publicly accessible and occasionally refined list of personal values.

  1. First, be kind.
  2. Be more generous.
  3. Be honest and clear.
  4. Be reliable, earn trust.
  5. Find energy in optimism.
  6. Believe in people.
